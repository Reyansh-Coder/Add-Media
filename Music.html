The History Of Music

Music has been a crucial part of human civilization, from early rituals to modern concerts. In ancient Egypt, Greece, and India, music was a key component of spiritual and social events.

During the Renaissance and Baroque periods, composers like Bach and Handel defined Western classical music. Later, composers like Beethoven revolutionized symphonic structure and musical storytelling.
